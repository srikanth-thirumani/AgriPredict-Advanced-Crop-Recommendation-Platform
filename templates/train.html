Train Machine Learning Models

Train the recommendation and production prediction models with the latest data to get accurate agricultural insights.

Model Training Process
1
Data Preprocessing

The system loads and preprocesses the crop recommendation and production datasets, cleaning and transforming the data for machine learning.

2
Training Crop Recommendation Model

A Random Forest Classifier is trained using parameters like nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall to predict the most suitable crop.

3
Training Production Prediction Model

A Random Forest Regressor is trained to estimate the expected production yield based on the crop type, area, and other factors.

4
Model Evaluation

Models are evaluated using cross-validation and performance metrics to ensure accuracy and reliability of predictions.

5
Model Saving

Trained models and their components are saved for future use in providing crop recommendations and production predictions.
